About Monster

Monster is an exciting survival game where players must navigate through increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Choose from unique characters with special abilities, collect power-ups, and upgrade your skills to survive as long as possible.

This addictive roguelike game offers endless replayability with procedurally generated levels and a variety of enemies to challenge your skills. Can you become the ultimate monster slayer?

Unique Characters

Choose from multiple characters, each with their own special abilities and playstyles to match your preferred strategy.

Power-Up System

Collect various power-ups during your run to enhance your abilities, increase your damage, or gain new special attacks.

Endless Challenge

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters with unique movement patterns and attacks. How long can you survive?

How to Play

Monster is easy to learn but challenging to master. Here's how to get started:

  1. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map.
  2. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies.
  3. Collect experience gems from defeated monsters to level up.
  4. Choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones when you level up.
  5. Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters.
